Biography

I'm a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK), under the supervision of Prof. Evangeline F.Y. Young since Fall 2018. Previously, I received my bachelor degree in Software Engineering from Shandong University (SDU) and my master degree in Computer Science and Technology from Beihang University (BUAA). My research interests include machine learning on design for manufacturability and physical design in VLSI CAD.
